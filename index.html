This application is operated by Arawak Supply Co for personal and organizational use only. It is used solely to publish product images and pins to Pinterest boards to promote Arawak Supply Co designs.

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users or third parties. The app only accesses the Pinterest account of the app owner to create and schedule pins.
